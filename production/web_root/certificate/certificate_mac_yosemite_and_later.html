Workflow Products, LLC Certificate
Mac OS X

Step: 1

Download the Workflow Products, LLC Root Certificate by clicking the following link:Download


Step: 2

Navigate to your Downloads folder and open the certificate.


Step: 3

Go into the certificate category.


Step: 4

Right click (or Ctrl-click if you have a one-button mouse) the root certificate and click get info.


Step: 5

Open the "Trust" section.


Step: 6

In the first drop down, select always trust.


Step: 7

Close the window.


Step: 8

Type in your name and password.


The Workflow Products, LLC Root Certificate is installed.
Note: You may need to quit your browser for the changes to take effect.
