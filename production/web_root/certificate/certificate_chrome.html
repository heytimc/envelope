Workflow Products, LLC Certificate
Google Chrome on Windows

Step: 1

Download the Workflow Products, LLC Root Certificate by clicking the following link: Download


Step: 2

Go to Google Chrome settings and click "Show Advanced Settings". Then scroll to the bottom of the settings and find the "HTTPS/SSL" settings and click "Manage Certificates...".


Step: 3

The "Certificates" window will pop up. Click "Import..." then click "Next".


Step: 4

The "Certificate Import Wizard" will pop up. Click "Browse".


Step: 5

Navigate to your downloads folder and open "workflowproducts.crt".


Step: 6

Click Next.


Step: 7

Click "Browse...".


Step: 8

Select "Trusted Root Certification Authorites" and click OK.


Step: 9

Click "Next".


Step: 10

Follow the prompts then click "Finish".


Step: 11

A "Security Warning" will pop up. It should present you with the number "42B88485 6CB97B31 5D6423F8 960351E7 C7AD8BF1". If so then click "Yes".


Step: 12

Completely close out and reopen Chrome for the changes to take effect.


Done.

The Workflow Products, LLC Root Certificate is installed.
